Our Story

Passion for Pakistani Cuisine

Dastarkhwan was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Pakistan to our guests. Our journey began in the heart of Lahore, inspired by generations of culinary tradition.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, traditional cooking techniques, and recipes passed down through generations to create dishes that remind you of home.
